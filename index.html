Metabolic analysis based on
fingerprints of Angelica sinensis

Angelica refers to the dry roots of Angelica sinensis, an herb of Chinese traditional medicine. It has a widely curative effect in modern medicine. Because the internal chemistry components of Angelica sinensis are very complex and are lastly affected by different environmental factors and human factors during it growth cycle, it is difficult to control the plucking time and its dose effect. In my bachelor thesis, a systematic research on Angelica sinensis’ metabolomics was conducted at 27 samples in the different growth stages from Minxian, Gansu Province.



当归

The bio-fingerprint information of Angelica sinensis in different growth period was extracted by means of H-NMR and GC-MS. The experimental results of H-NMR and GC-MS are shown in following figures. The statistical test, such as variance analysis and t-test, was implemented to distinguish the chemical characteristic peaks and chemical retention time of Angelica sinensis which varies greatly during the growth period.


   
The result of H-NMR
    
The result of GC-MS

In order to discover the dynamic accumulation of angelica metabolites and explore the metabolic components which are sensitive to the changes of years in its growth cycle, orthogonal partial least squares discriminant analysis (OPLS-DA) is used to analyze the integral relative content in spectral data systematically. By comparing with database, the characteristic signal peaks of linoleic acid, Angelica polysaccharide, ligustilide, ferulic acid, Angelica lactone A, butenyl phthalide and 3-n-Butylphthalide were identified on the H-NMR fingerprint. According to the Universal Database of Gas Chromatographic Retention Index, furthermore, we identified about 44 volatile compounds from the volatile oil of Angelica sinensis. Identification results are shown in following figures.



   
Substance Attribution of spectral data

The experiment results show that: Z-ligustilide, ferulic acid, and other three components are active pharmaceutical ingredients , and the changes of polysaccharide,15 alkanol, n-butylidenephthalide and other nonpharmacodynamic components are also obvious in three years of growth period. Therefore those compounds can be the symbolic chemical compositions used to evaluate the quality of angelica and control the harvest time; In the experimental process, we had found that the medicinal ingredients in angelica sinensis don’t reach the maximum content in the traditional harvest time, but the ratio between the Z-ligustilide and other ingredients, such as n-butylidenephthalide, reaches a reasonable level.

The analysis result of GC-MS

The applied research can guide the picking period of Angelica sinensis and the extraction of effective medicinal ingredients. Finally, this bachelor paper entitled Metabolic Analysis Based on Fingerprint of Angelica Sinensis stood out among 350 papers of the whole college and later incorporated in the Excellent Graduation Thesis Collection of 2017 Undergraduates of Central South University.

Community Detection Based on
Heterogeneous Network of Statistical Researches

1. Research Background

Heterogeneous network is different from homogeneous network, which converts the network information extracted from the complex systems into the different types of nodes and links. At present, the majority of community detection methods are worked on homogeneous networks and such a hypothesis that the nodes and the links of a certain network structure are all the identical type is made. That hypothesis provides much convenience for the study on the community detection method. Compared with the community detection in homogeneous networks, the community detection based on heterogeneous networks can find out more association information and more accurate community structure. The research object of this paper is just based on the heterogeneous network which is constructed by using the academic paper data in the statistics research field.


2. Research methods

Data collection of statistical research literatures: by using “beautifulsoup” and “selenium” module in python, I dynamically crawled and collected all literature data about statistics and probability at the database of “web of science” to construct the thesis topic network.


Information page in “Web of Science”

Because Impact Factor of some journals is too small, those journals are unrepresentative. Therefore, the textual data in this thesis are fixed in those papers from 27 famous statistical and probabilistic journals which have high IF and top rank in JCR. Those selected journals include Annals of Statistics, American Statistician, Biometrics, Journal of Statistical Software, etc. And every paper in those journals has many variables, such as “authors”, “ citation count”,” references”,”abstract”,etc. A sample data is shown in the following chart.


A sample data

More detailed data examples and the code of this network data acquisition are stored in this code repository.


Creation and cleaning of data structure. The feature of every literature was saved and then extracted for creating the data nodes——journal node, paper node and writer node respectively. For the data structure of attributes of every node, I saved it by the json tree structure so as to be called conveniently. What’s more, normalization cleaning and disposal are used in textual data to keep the data format identical for modeling.


The construction for heterogeneous network. There are three layers in the heterogeneous network including journal layer, paper layer and writer layer. Among the layers, some adjacent edges with same type exist. The adjacent edges in paper layer represent relation of mutual reference and the edges in writer layer represent cooperative relation. Between the journal layer and paper layer there exist adjacent edge representing relation, and so do the paper layer and writer layer. In such way, a three-dimensional network of statistics research was formed. The detailed network model is illustrated in following figure.


Heterogeneous network in this paper

The community detection of journal layer based on LDA topic model.This process is for the community detection in the journal layer merely. Each journal node contains the abstract information of these papers published in it from 2013 to 2018. The corpus and dictionary of all literatures are established for modeling LDA topic model. According to membership degree of every journal node for each theme, the research field, namely, the community for every journal will be detected. The result of LDA is illustrated in the following figure.
The result of LDA

The community detection based on label propagation model in paper layer.According to the adjacent edge effect existing in interlayers, the paper layer is divided into several paper sub-networks firstly. The community detection will be implemented in these sub-networks by parallel operation in these sub-networks. There is information in these sub-networks, like keyword, abstract and publisher and so on. Referring to these attributes, the similarity among the nodes was defined and the probability of propagation among the nodes was calculated further with those similarity. At last, the community detection should be implemented by propagation probability matrix of label propagation algorithm. The result of community detection in this layer is shown in following picture.


The paper layer has been divided into several paper sub-networks according to the LDA result


the result of community detection in paper layer

The community detection based on label propagation model in author layer.According to the adjacent edge effect existing in interlayers, the author layer is divided into several author sub-networks firstly. The community detection will be implemented in these sub-networks by parallel operation in these subnets. There is information in each node of these sub-networks, including keyword, abstract and university and so on. Referring to these attributes, the similarity among the nodes was defined and the probability of propagation among the nodes was calculated further. At last, the community detection should be implemented by propagation probability matrix of label propagation algorithm.
The result of community detection in author layer


3. Research result

Through the community detection methods, such as LDA topic model and label propagation algorithm, multi-dimensional structure, multimode information, semantic information and link connection in three dimensional networks will be extracted and utilized to seek out the community structures which are relatively close and have the practical significance. In the empirical analysis section, by using the method presented by this paper, multidimensional scientific research communities which have relatively compact structure, higher modularity and explicit practical significance are found out, including 4 journal communities, 20 paper communities under those 4 journal communities, and 92 author communities under those 20 paper communities.

The scientific research communities detected can provide science researchers with the journals, research achievements and research collaborators in the same field and can enable them to grasp the research direction and progress in future. Compared with traditional methods of homogeneous community detection, such as Louvain, the method proposed by the paper can make the most of heterogeneous information and node attribute information contained in the statistical science research network, find out the overlapping communities and cope with complex relationship of multi-dimensional structure well to improve the accuracy of algorithm. Moreover, such method can reduce the complexity of operation time for community detection with the parallel processing.
